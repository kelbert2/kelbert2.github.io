  • Parallelized Basal Ganglia Model for GPGPUs
    C++, CUDA

    By parallelizing a model of the basal ganglia region of the brain, we can make experiments concerning the treatment of tremors quicker and more widely accessible.

    Worked on writing and parallelyzing a model of the network of neurons in the Basal Ganglia brain region. Written in CUDA and C++ to run on GPGPUs, or General Purpose Graphics Processing Units, to dramatically decrease simulation time and increase the number of neurons able to be simulated in the system. Deep brain stimulation to this region of the brain has been shown to reduce motor symptoms of Parkinson's, including tremors. By creating an efficient and scalable multi-threaded program, we open up an incredible resource to researchers with limited coding experience and those with the C++ experience needed to build on top of our model.
